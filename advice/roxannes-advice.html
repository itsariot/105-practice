Hi my name is Roxanne and I have some advice for you!

Don't Give Up!

If at first you don't succeed, try, try again. Keep going until you get it!

Practice makes perfect. You can't get better if you don't practice and try to do better each time.

Don't be afraid to take breaks.

Never underestimate taking a break and giving your mind a rest. If you're struggling and just can't get it, get up and take a walk, make some tea, let your brain unwind and relax.

Sometimes we want to keep pushing ourselves and while that can help sometimes, giving your mind a rest allows you to come back to the project with a fresh mindset.

Look at you now, here you are working on writing code! Could you do this yesterday? No... so you're already improving aren't you? Keep up the good work, you're awesome!
